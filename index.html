ABOUT SRILANKA

The main island of the island nation of Sri Lanka, itself known as Sri Lanka is a South Asian island in the Indian Ocean,Natural resources include limestone, graphite, mineral sands, gems, phosphates, clay, hydropower.Sri Lankan cuisine has been shaped by many historical, cultural and other factors. Contact with foreign traders who brought new food items,Today, some of the staples of Sri Lankan cuisine are rice, coconut and spices. The latter are used due to the country's history as a spice producer and trading post over several centuries.


The culture of Sri Lanka mixes modern elements with traditional aspects and is known for its regional diversity. Sri Lankan culture has long been influenced by the heritage of Theravada Buddhism passed on from India,Sri Lankan culture is internationally associated with distinct cuisine,exports such as tea, cinnamon, and gemstones, as well as a robust tourism industry.One of the many reasons as to why people visit Sri Lanka is because of its very diverse wildlife.A lot of nature reserves and santuaries are in Sri Lanka in order to protect the wildlife from human interests that might harm the animals including poaching. Visitors are allowed in to these reserves where you can take safari trips with a trained guide provided by the reserves themselves.
